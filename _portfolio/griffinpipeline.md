Griffin-Pipeline provides a reproducible and modular workflow for preprocessing and analyzing Oxford Nanopore Technologies (ONT) sequencing data, including basecalling, quality assessment, and taxonomic classification. Its goal is to enhance research reproducibility by clearly documenting each step and relying on widely available open-source tools.

For details, visit the <a href="https://github.com/Snyder-Institute/Griffin-Pipeline" target="_blank">GitHub</a> for more information.
